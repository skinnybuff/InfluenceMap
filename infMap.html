The Influence Map Web App

The analog map was originally developed by fox-orian. The Web app developed by Shaun White & Elrick Ryan.

As an artist, you are influnced by things. The reason why you do what you do is the combined application of all the things that influence you. If you run into a major block and cannot create a new work, chances are you are forgetting what inspires you and need a refresher. Add information for every thing that influences you and fuels the art you love to create. Place the links and rearrange them in order of prominence. The squares will be populated and you can refer to it as a kind of "map" in the future incase you get lost.
